The Story of ProductHubNepal

We are a small team of passionate tech enthusiasts based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Frustrated by the complexities of typical online shopping carts, delivery delays, and grey-market imports, we built ProductHubNepal.

We work directly with authorized distributors and reliable international importers to source 100% genuine electronics: from the latest iPhones and MacBooks to studio-grade headphones, cameras, and gaming consoles.

By combining conversational order processes on WhatsApp with swift, hand-delivered shipping, we guarantee a premium hardware checkout experience. No cart delays. No checkout drama. Just authentic tech assets in your hands.
